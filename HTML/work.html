Workshop

Lexuz Workshop is a fully integrated car workshop in Pluit, Indonesia, that offers Preventive, Corrective, and Predictive Maintenance services for both electric and gasoline vehicles. Lexuz Workshop offers high-quality car maintenance services such as servicing, check-ups, and modifications. Customers have entrusted their vehicles to Lexuz Workshop due to the friendliness of staff and the excellence of service.

Our team is made up of competent and trained mechanics, who specialize in gasoline engines and electrical engines. Our technical staff has hands-on, multi-dimensional experience in the maintenance of a wide range of gasoline and electric vehicles. The Lexuz Workshop base and workshop facilities in Pluit are fully equipped with modern equipment to produce approximately 100 vehicles per month.

periodic service

This includes changing the oil and other fluids in the vehicle on a regular basis. Every car already has recommendations that should be followed. Oil, oil filter, air filter, brake fluid, engine coolant, spark plug, etc.

Modification

Not always workshops only deal with repairs. Modifications are provided for those who want to change the appearance of the vehicle to be more beautiful. Wheels, body kits, audio, window film, exhaust, etc.

General Checkup

Is a thorough check on the car. Generally done when someone wants to buy a used car and make sure the condition of the car is healthy. What is meant is, of course, to find out existing deficiencies or damage.

Body Repair

Identical to painting a damaged or dented car body. Body panels that are heavily damaged must of course be replaced and finished with painting to match the colors.
